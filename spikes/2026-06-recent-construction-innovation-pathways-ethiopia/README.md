# Recent Construction Innovation Pathways in Ethiopia

This spike compares model-generated research outputs on recent or emerging construction-material and building-system innovations in Ethiopia.

The purpose is to understand what newer systems have reached prototype, pilot, commercial, or institutional traction after the IHDP / ribbed-HCB era, and what adoption pathways they appear to be using.

## Focus Areas

- Kubik / recycled plastic building components
- Agrostone and other prefabricated panels
- CSEB / Hydraform / earthen masonry
- Light-gauge steel and prefabricated housing
- 3D printing and container housing
- LC3 / low-carbon cement
- Scoria concrete
- Bamboo or engineered bamboo systems
- Vaulted earthen floor systems

## Workflow

1. Archive the shared prompt.
2. Archive raw model generations.
3. Compare model outputs.
4. Extract cleaned cross-model findings.
5. Add human/local review notes.
6. Produce reviewed findings.

## Important Rule

Raw model generations are research leads, not accepted findings.
