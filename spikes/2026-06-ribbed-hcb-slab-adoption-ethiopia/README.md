# Ribbed / HCB Slab Adoption in Ethiopia

This spike compares independent model-generated research outputs on ribbed / HCB slab adoption in Ethiopian condominium and low-cost housing construction.

The purpose is to identify what the models agree on, what sources each found, where claims conflict, and what needs human or local expert verification.

## Workflow

1. Archive the shared prompt.
2. Archive raw generations from ChatGPT 5.5, Gemini Pro Extended, and Claude.
3. Compare the three model outputs.
4. Extract cleaned cross-model findings.
5. Add human review notes.
6. Produce reviewed findings.

## Important Rule

Raw model generations are research leads, not accepted findings.
