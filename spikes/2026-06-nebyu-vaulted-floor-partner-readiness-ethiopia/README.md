# Nebyu Vaulted Floor Partner Readiness in Ethiopia

This spike investigates what Nebyu Samuel Haile should understand and prepare before approaching potential partners about a first monitored building using his vaulted earthen floor system in Ethiopia.

The focus is not whether the system should be adopted.

The focus is institutional feasibility:

- how universities, NGOs, donors, engineers, contractors, and government-linked actors may interpret the system
- what evidence they would need before engagement
- what blocks approval before technical merit becomes relevant
- what kind of first building could generate useful evidence
- whether a partner discovery deck is sufficient or whether other artifacts are needed
